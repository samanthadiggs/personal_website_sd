Kind

During my time as a intern I volunteered at the Salem Sound Coast Watch, an environmental organization. Salem Sound Coastwatch is an environmental organization. The service I did was go around specific streets in Salem looking for storm drains. Once we found a storm drain we cleaned the area surrounding it and inside it, if we could. Then, we spray painted a message next to the drain facing the sidewalk stating something along the lines of all drains lead to the ocean. We spent the day cleaning up stormdrains and writing a message next to it reminding people that what they put in the storm drains goes to the ocean.
