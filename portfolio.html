the pokeball project

The “Pokéball” is a fictional item to capture Pokémon from the famous franchise “Pokémon”. There are several aspects to the ball itself, with flashing lights to indicate a captured or escaped Pokémon and having the Pokéball reopen when the Pokémon escapes. We set out to create a toy Pokéball with these functions, ultimately implementing a game and entertainment along with it. Our Pokéball could be marketed as a real-life Pokéball to capture these fun creatures for Pokémon enjoyers.
